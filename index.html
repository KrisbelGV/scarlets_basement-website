What is Scarlet's basement and how does it work?

It acts as an intermediary between the public information provided by Scratch and our users, adding an extra layer of filtering. The service offers a set of exclusive and complementary utilities that we hope you will find useful.

Is the project associated with Scratch?

No. It is an independent tool developed by KrisbelGV. Its long-term maintenance is neither defined nor ruled out, so we recommend that our users stay tuned for the latest updates.

Who is Scarlet?

She is my OC (original character), illustrated by me. She is not one of the characters registered by MIT, and any connection to them in their stories is purely fan fiction.

What are your privacy policies?

It adheres to high security standards, in line with the educational platform, using a rate limiting system and IP hashing for anonymity. If you disagree with these policies, please cancel your use immediately. You can modify the other options in your browser's settings.

We'd love to hear your opinions and suggestions. Please post them in our official Scratch forum thread, and we'll do your best to answer your questions as soon as possible.
